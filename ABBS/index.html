FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ABBS

What is ABBS and how does it work?

ABBS is a comprehensive subscription management platform that helps you track, manage, and optimize all your subscriptions in one place. It connects to your accounts to automatically identify subscriptions, notifies you of upcoming renewals, and helps you discover better deals.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your payment information. We do not store your actual card details on our servers, but rather use tokenization and other secure methods to manage your payment data.

How much does ABBS cost?

ABBS offers a freemium model. The basic version is completely free to use with limited features. Premium plans will be available starting from €4.99/month with additional features like advanced analytics, unlimited subscriptions, priority support, and more.

Can I manage both digital and physical subscriptions?

Yes! That's one of our key differentiators. ABBS allows you to manage both digital subscriptions (like streaming services, software, etc.) and physical subscriptions (like gym memberships, magazine subscriptions, etc.) all in one platform.

When will ABBS be available?

We're planning to launch the beta version in Q4 2025, with the full release scheduled for Q1 2026. Join our waitlist to get early access and exclusive benefits when we launch!
